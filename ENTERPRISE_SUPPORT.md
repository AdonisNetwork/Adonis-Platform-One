This Enterprise Support Policy defines the support, maintenance, and service levels available to commercial customers of Adonis Platform One under the Adonis Commercial License Agreement (ACLA-1.0).

---

## 1. Support Channels

Enterprise customers receive access to the following channels:

- **Dedicated Support Portal**
- **Email support**
- **Priority ticketing system**
- **Security alert notifications**
- **Maintenance release updates**
- **Feature request submission**

Optional (add-on):
- **Dedicated Slack/Teams channel**
- **Technical account manager (TAM)**

---

## 2. Severity Levels & Response Times (SLA)

| Severity Level | Description | Initial Response | Workaround | Resolution Target |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **S1 – Critical** | Production outage, data corruption, or major failure preventing use | ≤ 2 hours | ≤ 4 hours | ≤ 3 business days |
| **S2 – High** | Critical feature degraded but system still operational | ≤ 4 hours | ≤ 1 day | ≤ 7 business days |
| **S3 – Medium** | Non-critical bug, minor malfunction or UI/UX defect | ≤ 1 business day | N/A | ≤ 30 business days |
| **S4 – Low** | Enhancement request or documentation issue | ≤ 3 business days | N/A | N/A |
