These Ordering Terms (“AOT”) govern the purchase, subscription, licensing, OEM integration, and enterprise support services for the Adonis Platform One software (“Software”) provided by Adonis Network (“Vendor”).

These terms apply to all orders, quotes, purchase agreements, invoices, renewal forms, and written commercial contracts unless replaced by a mutually signed agreement.

## 2. Order Types

The following order types are available:

**(a) Subscription Order**  
Grants time-limited access to Commercial Use, SaaS deployment, support, and updates.

**(b) OEM Order**  
Grants licenses for embedding the Software into hardware or commercial products.

**(c) Perpetual License Order**  
One-time payment for perpetual Commercial Use rights (excludes cloud services & support).

**(d) Enterprise Support Order**  
Provides enterprise-grade support, SLAs, and maintenance services.

**(e) Add-On Order**  
Adds optional modules, feature packs, security components, or OEM extensions.

## 3. Quote & Purchase Process

1. Customer requests formal quote (RFQ)
2. Vendor issues commercial quote
3. Customer submits purchase order (PO) or executes Order Form
4. Vendor confirms order and invoicing schedule
5. Services, licenses, and deliverables commence upon receipt of payment or as contractually agreed

All quotes are valid for **30 calendar days**, unless otherwise specified.
